Trainee Commons Printer Drivers

Installation:

Install the printer drivers according to your OS.

Download Window (64-bit) Drivers

Download Mac OSX Drivers

For Windows devices:

Open the file and extract it in your Program Files by clicking "Browse" and opening "My Computer".

Click "Windows (C:)" and then "Program Files" and extract the file.

Open "Devices and Printers by finding it on your start menu or searching for it. Then do the following:

1. Add a printer

2. Add a network, wireless or Bluetooth printer

3. Find "RICOH MP 2501" and click next

For Mac OS:

Open System Prefrences in the Apple menu.

Click "Print & Scan"

Click the plus (+) to add a printer

Find and add the "RICOH MP 2501" pinter

You should now be able to print from your laptop.

Scanning:

After you install the printer drivers, the programs on your laptop should be able to use the Trainee Commons Printer as a scanner.

Follow the instructions in your program of choice and "RICOH MP 2501" should be an option to use to scan.
